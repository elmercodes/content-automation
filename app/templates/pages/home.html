Local-first social publishing

Draft once, review safely, and keep a local publishing ledger

This app runs entirely on your machine. Create a master post, keep ordered local media, review per-platform output, submit where direct posting is supported, and keep a lightweight local history without leaving the browser or adding JavaScript.

Start locally

Use the same server-rendered workflow for both single-image posts and ordered image carousels.

  • Compose saves one master post with ordered media items
  • Platforms shows configured platforms only
  • Platform review renders local previews before submission
  • History keeps a durable local ledger of saved posts and outcomes

Current provider support

  • X can be previewed and posted directly when all four X credentials are set locally
  • Instagram can be made visible for review with a local access token, but direct posting is deferred
  • Facebook can be made visible for review with a local page ID, but direct posting is deferred
  • Carousel support is image-only in this workflow, with platform-specific limits enforced before submission
